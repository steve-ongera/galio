Frequently Asked Questions
How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. You'll find tracking information and status updates for all your orders.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Please contact us to initiate a return.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days within Kenya. Express shipping options are available for faster delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash on delivery for eligible orders.

Can I change or cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it, provided it hasn't been shipped. Please contact us immediately for order modifications.
